EdCamps Inc.

Summer Educational Camps for Kids Ages 8-14

Our Philosophy

At EdCamps, we believe that learning should be fun no matter the subject. That's why we've teamed up with incredible camp counselors and organized some awesome classes and activities that will have your camper learning life-long skills, while enjoying themselves at the same time!

Choose from over 15 innovative technology classes and outdoor activities for campers between the ages of 10-14 to engage in. We offer a variety of 2-week camp sessions in various locations across the United States.

Intro to JavaScript

We offer an introductory JavaScript course tailored for the ages of our campers so that they can coding and collaboration skills. As campers become more experienced and outgrow the material, our curriculum will evolve to include more advanced concepts and meet their needs.

Image above from techno-geek.tk

Learn to Swim & Surf

Never ridden a wave before? No problem! We offer surfing lessons for campers at every level of experience. We take campers to Santa Cruz Beach where there is a variety of wave sizes that will meet all campers needs. After a day well spent in the ocean, we will stop by our favorite lunch spot on the boardwalk!

Image above from iconfinder.com

Intro to App Design

Have a great app idea? We'll help you make that idea come to life! With an introductory app design course we will teach campers innovative skills that they will be able to take with them into high school and beyond! We offer courses tailored towrards both Android and iOS application development.

Locations

Edcamps Inc. hosts camps in various places throughout the United States. Most of our camps are held in the San Francisco Bay Area, but we also have camp locations in Nebraska, Florida, and New York. Our most popular camp is EdCamps Santa Clara, where students are able to use Santa Clara University's facilities and classrooms.
